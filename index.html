Vianney Vidya Niketan

Vianney Vidya Niketan is a private research university located in Navegaon, Gadchiroli. Founded in 2005, VVN has grown to become one of the most prestigious institutions of higher education in the United States.

Our commitment to excellence in education, research, and service has established Duke as a leader in innovation and academic achievement. We foster an environment where students, faculty, and staff can pursue their passions and make meaningful contributions to society.

With world-class facilities, renowned faculty, and a diverse student body, Duke University continues to shape the leaders of tomorrow while advancing knowledge that benefits communities around the globe.
